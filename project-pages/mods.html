Minecraft Mods
My Minecraft Mods are addons to the cult classic Better Than Wolves Mod, enhancing the experience with additional content, balance changes, and quality of life improvements.
Stuff & Things
Stuff & Things is my biggest project so far. It is a collection of seemingly independent additions that change the balance of the game to reward cautious strategies, and to slightly ease the otherwise stressful gameplay of the BTW early game. The latest update extends BTW's Hardcore Darkness to the Nether, which combined with Dynamic Lighting creates a truly unique experience. Find out more about it on the Better Than Wolves Forum thread, or browse the source code in the GitHub repository!
Boat Mapping
Boat Mapping is the most popular quality of life change from Stuff & Things, availale as a standalone addon for those who want to use it without the rest of my changes. It allows players to easily explore maps while sailing in boats. It is updated to the latest version of Better Than Wolves Community Edition, and it uses mixins to ensure compatibility with other addons. Find out more about it on the Better Than Wolves Forum thread, or browse the source code in the GitHub repository!
